How to use

Auto OCR reads the page with no settings: line positions, baselines, font, size and spacing are measured from the pixels, and every line carries a byte-exactness certificate (green = certified). Redaction boxes and rules are detected and shown as overlays. Auto OCR All + Download .txt / .json export the whole document. Click a row to edit its text; double-click any glyph box to extract it.
